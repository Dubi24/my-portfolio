About Kennojay

At Kennojay Oil and Gas, we are dedicated to powering industries, businesses, and communities with high-quality petroleum products.
Since our establishment, we have built a reputation for delivering premium diesel, engine oil, petroleum, and natural gas in large quantities—backed by efficient logistics that ensure location is never a barrier.
Our mission is to provide dependable and efficient energy solutions through the supply of superior petroleum products, while upholding integrity, safety, and excellence in every transaction.
Our vision is to become a leading name in the global oil and gas industry, recognized for innovation, trust, and unmatched customer satisfaction in energy supply and distribution.
